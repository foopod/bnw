A collection of Black and White Photographs.

Aotea Square. A summers day. Food vendors serve anything from Tteokbokki to Churros. Pigeons flock hoping to collect anything left behind.

Pedestal lit dancers perform at Turama, Albert Park. A festival of immersive arts and performance.

A boy marches down the streets of Auckland to fight for Animal Rights.

A small girl enjoys her smoothie while taking in all the sights and sounds of the Auckland Night Markets.

The Tallest Ghosts. The morning fog swirls around the trunks of pine trees. Now hundreds of years old, they are marked to be chopped down.
